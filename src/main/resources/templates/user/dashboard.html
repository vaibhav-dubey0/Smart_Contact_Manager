Welcome to Dashboard Page

Manage your profile, contacts, messages, and feedback efficiently.

1. Profile Page

View logged-in user information, including profile photo, name, email, and phone verification status.

2. Add Contacts

Create new contacts by providing details like name, phone number, and email.

3. Contacts Field

View, update, delete, and filter created contacts. Export contact details as needed.

4. Direct Message

Send direct messages to stored contacts via their email addresses.

5. Log Out

Securely log out of your account with a single click.

6. Feedback

Provide feedback for any specific user by entering their contact name, email ID, and feedback message.
